Guidelines

The Alumni Connect platform is designed to foster mentorship, networking, and career guidance between students and alumni in a professional environment. Students should approach alumni with respect, clearly state their requests, and be mindful of their time. They should use the platform for career-related inquiries, networking, and mentorship while maintaining professional communication. Alumni are encouraged to offer guidance, share industry insights, and provide networking opportunities while maintaining appropriate boundaries. Both students and alumni should avoid spamming, solicitation, or sharing personal information without consent. Communication should be clear, respectful, and timely. Participants are encouraged to engage in networking events, mentorship programs, and discussions while ensuring privacy and security. Any misuse of the platform, such as harassment or unprofessional behavior, should be reported for necessary action. By adhering to these guidelines, the Alumni Connect platform can serve as a valuable tool for career growth, professional networking, and meaningful alumni-student relationships.

Terms and Conditions

By using the **Alumni Connect** platform, users agree to follow the established terms and conditions to ensure a respectful and professional networking environment. The platform is strictly for students, alumni, faculty, and authorized representatives, and users must provide accurate profile information. All interactions should be professional, focusing on mentorship, career guidance, and networking, with no solicitation, spamming, or unrelated promotions allowed. Users must communicate respectfully, avoiding harassment, offensive content, or any form of discrimination, while also respecting privacy by not sharing personal information without consent. The platform administrators reserve the right to monitor activities and remove inappropriate content. Any advice or information shared should be truthful, relevant, and not misleading. The institution is not responsible for job placements, business dealings, or personal disputes arising from platform interactions, and users should exercise discretion while networking. Violations of these terms may result in warnings, restricted access, or permanent removal. The institution also reserves the right to update these terms as needed. By accessing **Alumni Connect**, users acknowledge and agree to these conditions, ensuring a constructive and valuable experience for all members..
